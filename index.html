The Plastic Waste Crisis

In Dhaka, 646 tonnes of plastic waste are collected daily, comprising ~10% of the city's total waste. Nationwide, only ~21% of single-use bottles are recycled, leaving ~79% mismanaged. We're transforming this crisis into opportunity.

How It Works

Our Smart Plastic Recycling Hub Process

1

Localized Hubs

Mini recycling hubs for efficient collection, sorting, and processing of plastic waste in communities.

2

IoT Smart Bins

Utilizing IoT-enabled bins for precise waste tracking and real-time monitoring of collection data.

3

Community Engagement

Partnering with municipalities and schools for awareness drives and education programs.

4

Buy-Back System

Community earns cash for returned plastics, incentivizing participation in recycling efforts.
